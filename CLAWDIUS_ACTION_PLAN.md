## Risk Management

**If token launch flops:**
- Continue building product (fundamentals don't change)
- Token is bonus, not dependency

**If MVP takes longer:**
- Launch with fewer features (transcription only)
- Add AI summaries in v1.1

**If no users sign up:**
- Pivot to different productivity tool (Perplexity alternative)
- Or different market (US instead of EU)

**If legal issues arise:**
- Pause token utility, operate as normal SaaS
- Refund token holders OR pivot to different utility
