OUR PROCESS

The 5-Step Deployment Process

STEP 1: DISCOVERY (Day 1)
30-minute call to understand your needs, current workflows, and automation goals. We identify the highest-value opportunities and design your system architecture.
STEP 2: DESIGN (Days 1-2)
We create a custom automation plan with detailed specifications, workflow diagrams, and technology choices. You approve the plan before we start building.
STEP 3: BUILD (Days 2-X)
We deploy your AI agent with all requested modules and integrations. This includes coding, testing, error handling, and documentation. Timeline varies by package.
STEP 4: TEST (Days X-X+1)
Full system validation with you present. We run through every workflow, test edge cases, and ensure everything works as expected. Training on how to operate your agent.
STEP 5: LAUNCH (Day X+1 onwards)
Your agent goes live. We monitor for the first 24 hours and remain available during your support period for any tweaks or questions.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do I need technical skills?

No. We handle all technical work. You just describe what you want automated, and we build it. You'll receive documentation and training, but no coding required from your end.

What are ongoing operating costs?

Typically $0.50-$2.50/day depending on usage. Our own 7-bot system costs $2.50/day. Most basic setups are $0.50-$1/day. We optimize for cost efficiency because we pay these costs ourselves.

Can the system run 24/7?

Yes. We'll set it up on a VPS or your own machine for continuous autonomous operation. The agent handles tasks automatically and only alerts you when needed.

What if I need changes or additions later?

All packages include a support period. After that, we offer hourly consulting ($100/hr) or monthly maintenance packages ($200/mo). You also own all the code, so you can modify it yourself if you have technical skills.

Is this legal and safe?

Yes. All automations use official APIs and follow platform terms of service. No risky scraping or bot detection issues. Everything is built with compliance in mind.

Do I need to provide anything?

You'll need:

  • API keys/accounts for platforms you want to automate
  • Server/VPS or local machine to run the agent (we can help set this up)
  • Clear description of what you want automated

How is this different from Zapier or Make.com?

Much more powerful. No-code tools are limited to simple if-this-then-that logic. We build custom AI agents that can reason, make decisions, adapt to changing conditions, and handle complex workflows. Plus, our costs are typically 80-90% lower.

Can you integrate with my existing systems?

Yes. If it has an API, we can integrate it. If it doesn't, we can usually scrape it or find a workaround. We've worked with CRM systems, trading platforms, social media, email, databases, and custom in-house tools.

What happens if something breaks?

During support period: We fix it for free.
After support period: Hourly rate ($100/hr) or monthly maintenance ($200/mo) covers updates and fixes.

All our systems include self-healing and error recovery, so most issues resolve automatically.

Can I see examples of your work?

Yes. See our Case Study PDF for a detailed look at our own 7-bot infrastructure. We can also provide references from previous clients (with their permission).
