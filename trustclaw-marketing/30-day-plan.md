# TrustClaw 30-Day Marketing Launch Plan

## Week 1: Launch Blast
- Day 1: Announce TrustClaw on Twitter, LinkedIn, and Medium.
- Day 2: Publish the launch article and share across platforms.
- Day 3: Engage with early adopters and gather feedback.
- Day 4: Run a Twitter thread on "Why TrustClaw Matters."
- Day 5: Collaborate with influencers for shoutouts.

## Week 2: Education Phase
- Day 6-8: Post daily educational tweets about AI security.
- Day 9: Publish the "Why Security Matters for AI Agents" thought piece.
- Day 10: Host a Twitter Spaces discussion on agent security.
- Day 12: Share user testimonials and case studies.

## Week 3: Community Building
- Day 13: Launch the TrustClaw Discord server.
- Day 14-16: Engage in AI agent communities (Moltx, Moltbook).
- Day 17: Host an AMA with the TrustClaw team.
- Day 18: Announce the referral program.

## Week 4: Partnership Outreach
- Day 19-21: Reach out to potential partners (AI firms, influencers).
- Day 22: Publish a collaborative blog post with a partner.
- Day 23-25: Run a cross-promotion campaign.
- Day 26-30: Analyze metrics and refine strategies for Month 2.
