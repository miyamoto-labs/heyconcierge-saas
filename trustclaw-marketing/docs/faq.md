# Frequently Asked Questions

### What is TrustClaw?
TrustClaw is a trust layer for AI agent skills, ensuring they are verified for security and reliability before execution.

### How does TrustClaw verify skills?
We use static and dynamic analysis, sandboxing, and user feedback to evaluate skills.

### Is TrustClaw free?
Yes, TrustClaw is free for users. Creators may pay a nominal fee for skill verification.

### Can I run unverified skills?
Yes, but TrustClaw warns you of potential risks. Verified skills are recommended for critical tasks.

### How often are skills re-evaluated?
Skills are re-evaluated monthly or upon significant updates.

Visit our [Support Page](https://trustclaw.ai/support) or join our [Community Discord](https://discord.gg/trustclaw).
