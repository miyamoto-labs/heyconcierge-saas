# Submitting Your Skill for Verification

TrustClaw allows skill creators to submit their skills for verification, ensuring they meet security and reliability standards. Here’s how to get your skill verified:

## Submission Process
1. **Prepare Your Skill**: Ensure your skill adheres to TrustClaw’s [Security Guidelines](https://trustclaw.ai/guidelines).
2. **Submit for Review**: Navigate to the [TrustClaw Creator Portal](https://trustclaw.ai/creator) and upload your skill package.
3. **Await Verification**: Our team will review your skill within 48 hours.

## Verification Criteria
- **Code Integrity**: No malicious code or backdoors.
- **Data Privacy**: Compliance with data protection standards.
- **Performance**: Reliable execution without crashes.

## Post-Verification
- **Badge of Trust**: Verified skills receive a TrustClaw badge.
- **Marketplace Listing**: Your skill will be featured in the TrustClaw-verified section.
