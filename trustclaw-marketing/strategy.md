# TrustClaw Social Media Strategy

## Key Hashtags
- #OpenClaw
- #AIAgents
- #AISecurity
- #AgentSkills
- #TrustTheClaw

## Posting Schedule
- **Frequency**: 3-5 tweets per day, 2-3 LinkedIn/Medium posts per week.
- **Best Times**: 9 AM, 12 PM, 5 PM (GMT+1) for Twitter; 8 AM and 6 PM for LinkedIn.

## Engagement Strategy
- **Follow**: AI developers, security experts, and tech influencers.
- **Interact**: Reply to tweets about AI security, share relevant content, and participate in threads.
- **Community**: Engage with OpenClaw and AI agent communities on Discord and Telegram.

## Content Pillars
1. **Education**: Explain AI security risks and solutions.
2. **Product Updates**: Announce new features and skills.
3. **Community**: Highlight user stories and collaborations.
4. **Security Tips**: Share best practices for safe agent usage.
